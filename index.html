ABOUT

OakTrust Properties

Oak Trust Properties is a boutique, family owned property management business, headquartered in Summerville, South Carolina. Our purpose, whether you rent from us or hire us to manage your properties, is to give you peace of mind and help you to achieve your financial goals.

We provide our clients with a trustworthy, responsive and highly experienced team of experts that will always make decisions in your best interest to help you secure the best returns for your time and investment. Here at Oak Trust Properties, we promise you’ll get the attention you deserve, each and every time. We’ve got your back.

Oak Trust Properties exists to elevate the property management experience, by establishing genuinely dependable and trusting relationships. Our purpose is to represent the best interests of all parties involved–while providing best in class communication and seamless services. We are a company that genuinely loves what we do, and that is connecting all of the moving parts to provide you with the tools to optimize a successful investment.
